Al-Alam Mosque

Lalolara, Kambu, Kendari City, South East Sulawesi

The Al Alam Mosque is a mosque in Kendari City, Southeast Sulawesi Province (Southeast Sulawesi) which is nicknamed the floating mosque. The idea was initiated and construction began in 2010 during the leadership of the Governor of Southeast Sulawesi, Nur Alam. Inaugurated for use in 2018. The Al Alam Mosque is an icon of Kendari City which is visited by many local residents and immigrants.
